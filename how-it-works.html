How it Works

Organization

  • Sign-up as an organization
  • Create your project and start your campaign
  • Check your dashboard to see donations and volunteer requests.
    You can also edit your information or request a payout from the dashboard

Volunteers/Donors

  • Sign-up as an individual
  • Go to the “List of Entities” page to view organizations
    and causes that you might be interested in.
  • Click on your preferred entity to view more details Volunteer and/or donate
